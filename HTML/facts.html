Travel Facts

Here is a list of facts , advise and tips about traveling.

Facts

  • 1.Your brain is really stimulated to think differently and more creatively when you travel! Additionally, you're developing new neural connections in your brain, which might improve your ability to think and solve problems.
  • 2.Studies show that couples who travel together report heightened feelings of intimacy.
  • 3.Because of the elevation, the air around you gets drier, which exacerbates dry lips and knuckles.To get your total water loss, multiply the eight ounces you lose every hour by the length of your battle.

Advise

Here are advice that is given by people who have experience from traveling:

  • 1.Rise before dawn to avoid the large crowds of tourists and have the best locations to yourself. It's also a great time to capture images because of the soft, diffused light, and it's usually simpler to interact with people as they get ready for the day.
  • 2.If you want to see the parts of town where real people live and work, you need to go visit them. The best way to do this is on foot — without knowing exactly where you are going. Write down the name of your hotel so you can catch a taxi back if needed, then just pick a direction and start walking.
  • 3.There iss no need to be fluent in order to visit a foreign country. However, one tip I have learned is to master a few phrases in the local language can improve your travel experience. Hello, please, thank you, nice to meet you, excuse me, sorry, and “can I take your portrait” are some of my favorites.

Tips!

  • 1.Do not stick to a plan. Have one to guide you but do not follow it to heart as you may find things that will enjoy that you have not heard about!
  • 2.Rather than carrying different chargers for different countries , invest in a big and good quality power bank
  • 3. Do not over pack - so you have plenty of space to bring souvenirs back home!
